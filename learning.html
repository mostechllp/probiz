Structured Debate
Programs

Master the Art of Argumentation

Structured debate is a formal educational activity that develops rigorous thinking, articulate speech, and professional poise. Move beyond mere discussion and embrace a disciplined approach to exploring complex global issues.

EXPLORE OUR FORMATS

Debate Formats

Parliamentary Debate

Dynamic, fast-paced format emphasizing spontaneous wit and logical structure modeled after British Parliament.

Oxford-Style Debate

A competitive debate format on a single motion, where the audience votes on the winning side.

Lincoln-Douglas Debate

One-on-one format focusing on philosophical values and ethical frameworks behind current issues.

Public Forum Debate

Accessible and persuasive team debate centered on real-world news topics and evidence-based policy.

Why Debate?

Debate is not just a competition; it is a laboratory for leadership. It prepares individuals to handle complexity, respect dissent, and lead with clarity in high-pressure environments.
